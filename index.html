Questions you were going to ask

Frequently avoided answers.

Is there actual egg in Egg Water?
We prefer not to confirm or deny the egg. The egg's presence is best understood as a relationship rather than an ingredient.
Is it pasteurized?
Emotionally, yes. Egg Water has processed a great deal and arrives at your door at peace with itself.
Where is it sourced?
A single, undisclosed egg. We've found that naming the egg only invites further questions, none of which improve the product.
Is it safe to drink?
It is water. In the unlikely event that it is not water, it is still, at minimum, an experience.
Can I drink it every day?
We can't stop you, and after a certain subscription tier, we'd rather not.
What does the doneness actually change?
Your sense of control. The water remains, in all measurable respects, water. The firmness is a feeling we agree to share.
